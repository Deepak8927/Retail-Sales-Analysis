# 🛒 Retail Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify key revenue drivers, customer purchasing patterns, and opportunities to improve profitability. The goal is to transform raw data into actionable business insights that support decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Evaluate regional performance
* Detect trends over time
* Provide business recommendations

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* SQL (for data querying)
* Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Retail sales dataset (CSV format)
* Contains information on orders, products, categories, regions, sales, and profit

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Sales and profit analysis by category and region
* Time-based trend analysis
* Identification of high-performing and low-performing segments

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* The **Technology category** generates the highest revenue, indicating strong market demand
* Certain regions show **high sales but low profit**, suggesting pricing or discount issues
* Sales tend to increase during specific periods, indicating **seasonal trends**
* A small number of products contribute to a large portion of total sales (Pareto effect)

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing and inventory on high-performing categories
* Optimize pricing strategies in low-profit regions
* Increase stock during peak sales periods
* Identify and promote top-selling products to maximize revenue

---

## 📈 Future Improvements

* Build an interactive dashboard using Power BI
* Perform customer segmentation analysis
* Use predictive analysis for future sales forecasting

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis can uncover valuable insights and support better business decisions in the retail sector.
